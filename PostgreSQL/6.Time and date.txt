Date_trunc-
PostgreSQL date_trunc() function to truncate a timestamp or interval to a specified level of precision.
The date_trunc function truncates a TIMESTAMP or an INTERVAL value based on a specified date part e.g. ,
hour, week, or month and returns the truncated timestamp or interval with a level of precision.

Date_trunc('hour',TIMESTAMP'2020-10-01 05:15:45')
it will give 2020-10-01 05:00:00
